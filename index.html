Welcome to NeuroLab

You have been selected to participate in a controlled cognitive study conducted within a high-security experimental facility. NeuroLab monitors visual memory, pattern recognition and attention under varying conditions.

Each session adapts dynamically to your responses, testing both memory span and focus. Accuracy and reaction times are recorded for analysis, providing insights into cognitive performance.

Subjects should maintain concentration, as sequences may change unexpectedly. Only careful attention allows progression through increasingly complex stages of the experiment.
